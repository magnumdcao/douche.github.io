Recipes:

Sandwich Bread

Notes:

Ingredients:

  1. 240 ml, 40-celcius-degrees, water.
  2. 60 ml, 40-celcius-degrees, whole milk.
  3. 7 gram yeast.
  4. 25 granulated sugar.
  5. 60 gram, room-temperature, unsalted buffer.
  6. 1 and 1/2 teaspoons salt.
  7. 406 gram all-purpose flour.

High-level steps:

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Knead the dough for roughly 2 minutes.
  3. Let the dough rise for 1 - 2 hours.
  4. Punch down the dough to release air.
  5. Shape the dough into a large rectangle, then roll it into a loaf.
  6. Let the loaf rise for about 1 hour.
  7. Bake for 30-34 minutes until golden brown under 177 celcius degrees. Use a rack towards bottom!
